Ingredients:
 - 10 to 15 large prawns peeled and washed
 - 1 to 2 teaspoon red chili powder
 - 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
 - 1 tablespoon raw lemon juice
 - 1/2 cup corriander leaves chopped
 - 1 and 1/2 cup gram flour
 - 1/2 cup water
 - 1 teaspoon salt
 - 2 cups vegetable oil

Directions:
 1. Take a mixing bowl and add prawns, 1 and 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder, 1/2
    teaspoon turmeric powder, 1 tablespoon raw lemon juice and 1/2 teaspoon salt.
    Mix it well and keep it aside. Allow it to marinate for 15 mins.

 2. Take another mixing bowl and add gram flour, 1/2 teaspoon red chilipowder,
    1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder and 1/2 teaspoon salt and water. Keep adding small
    amount of water and mix it well till batter is smooth. The batter should not be
    too thin.

 3. Take a small wok add vegetable oil and heat.

 4. Now dip the prawns in the batter one at a time and put them into the wok,
	Deep fry till the skin looks mild reddish.

 5. Sprinkle chopped corriander and serve.
